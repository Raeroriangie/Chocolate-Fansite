Love, Chocolate

I have been to heaven before. It was the day I discovered chocolate.

Love on my lips, magic on my tongue; it was divine.

Blessed by it's holy flavour, my soul transcended.

I became consumed by the creaminess of white chocolate.

Bewitched by the dark magic of dark chocolate,

And possessed by every shade in between.


To you, Chocolate, I pledge my love.


There is no heaven greater than you.
